About Us

Where Expertise Meets Compassion

Expert Orthopedic Care by
Dr. Pradeep Lucas

Dr. Pradeep Lucas is a renowned orthopedic specialist at The Family Doctor in Bengaluru. With vast expertise in treating a wide range of orthopedic conditions, Dr. Lucas addresses issues such as Achilles tendon injuries, ankle sprains, bursitis, carpal tunnel syndrome, cerebral palsy, drop foot, hip and knee pain, spinal conditions, golfer's elbow, pelvic fractures, shoulder ailments, phantom limb pain, plantar fasciitis, sports injuries, arthritis, bone trauma, osteoporosis, and more. He offers comprehensive evaluation, treatment, and rehabilitation services, ensuring personalized and effective care for his patients. For expert orthopedic care, book an appointment with Dr. Pradeep Lucas today.
